
## Friends S01E01 - The Pilot

### Central Perk - Morning Coffee


- _Monica_: I just want to **break the ice** with this new coworker before our meeting tomorrow.
- _Joey_: You should just **hang out** with them after work, grab a coffee or something.
- _Chandler_: Yeah, once you get along with someone, everything at work gets easier.

#### Words and phrases


- **break the ice**: To initiate conversation or relieve tension in a social situation


 


- **hang out**: To spend time relaxing or socializing informally
